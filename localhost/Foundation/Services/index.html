Services, Trainings and Products
endorsed or accredited by the Foundation

seL4 is an attractive platform for building highly resilient systems, but to do so requires a fair degree of understanding of the technology. Fortunately, help is available from a growing number of community members offering products that ease system development and assurance, and provide training, consulting and development services around seL4.

The Foundation is working with members on developing certification programs for services, training and products. Until these are in place, we will provide provisional endorsements, which will lapse once the certification schemes are in place. We will support members with provisional endorsements in achieving full certification.

The certification (or endorsement for now) will indicate:

  • that a particular provider has the skills and experience to provide high-quality services to adopters of seL4;
  • that a particular training is of high quality and effective in providing useful skills for deploying the seL4 microkernel;
  • that a particular product is helpful to the community for building high-assurance seL4-based systems.
